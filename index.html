How to Use TruthScan AI

TruthScan AI is designed with simplicity and efficiency in mind. Follow these steps to analyze your videos:

  1. Step 1: Upload your video file using the upload section on the homepage. Supported formats include .mp4, .webm, and .ogg.
  2. Step 2: Once the video is uploaded, our AI model will process it frame by frame, looking for signs of manipulation or deepfake techniques.
  3. Step 3: After the analysis is complete, you'll receive a detailed result indicating whether the video is real or AI-generated, along with key insights about the detection.

Our platform ensures real-time processing and privacy. All video analysis is performed in your browser, meaning your data never leaves your device.

Need Help?

If you're experiencing issues with uploading or analyzing your video, or if you have questions about how to use the platform, our support team is here to assist you. Below are some common troubleshooting tips:
